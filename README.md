# The-Number: WhatsApp Validator & Email Sender
> **Mass Messaging. Active Lead Verification. Zero Friction.**

**The-Number** (hosted at [the-number.rf.gd](https://the-number.rf.gd)) is a specialized web utility designed for digital marketers and developers. It streamlines the process of verifying WhatsApp leads and managing email communications through a clean, centralized interface.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. 🛡️ WhatsApp Active Number Checker
Stop wasting resources on inactive leads. This tool integrates directly with professional APIs to filter your contact lists.
* **Bulk Validation:** Upload large lists of phone numbers and verify their WhatsApp status instantly.
* **API-Powered Accuracy:** Real-time checking to ensure high deliverability for your marketing campaigns.
* **Filtered Export:** After processing, download only the "Active" numbers in a clean format for your next broadcast.

### 2. 📧 Integrated Email Sender
A built-in module to handle outgoing communications directly from the dashboard.
* **Direct SMTP Integration:** Send emails without leaving the site.
* **Plaintext & HTML Support:** Perfect for quick alerts or formatted newsletters.
* **Seamless Workflow:** Verify your numbers and contact your leads in one continuous session.

## 📖 How to Use

### WhatsApp Checker:
1.  Navigate to the **WhatsApp Filter** section.
2.  Upload your contact list (supports `.txt` or `.csv` formats).
3.  Click **"Verify"** to run the API check.
4.  Download the **Filtered List** containing only active WhatsApp users.

### Email Suite:
1.  Open the **Email** module.
2.  Input the recipient's address, subject, and message.
3.  Hit **"Send"** to dispatch via the integrated mail server.
